ABOUT US

Our Story


Bruno Pizzeria has been satisfying cravings for 30 years. Our founder, Mr. Bruno opened the Bruno Pizzeria in May 1991 in Melbourne, Victoria, out of a passion for amazing pizzas.


Known across the country for its delectable Frontier, Italian Pizza's, Bruno Pizzeria is a name that spells exhilaration and satisfaction. With over three decades of journey in the hospitality industry, the brand has made a mark for itself. Owning 10 National Award to its credits, Bruno Pizzeria surely leaves an everlasting impression on one's mind and heart whenever one steps in.


We created this restaurant because we wanted to provide authentic Italian cuisine that offers customers a local experience. Our pizzas are curated with the finest of ingredients, fresh herbs and spices, with great attention paid to the recipes to evolve authentic flavours of the cuisine. Laden with culture, tradition and age-old warmth, BRUNO PIZZERIA is a experience that you will treasure for life.


We create food we're proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.
